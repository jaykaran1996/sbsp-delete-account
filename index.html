Delete Your Account – Suheldev Bharatiya Samaj Party App

You can request deletion of your account and data directly from within the app.

Steps:

  1. Open the SBSP app
  2. Go to the navigation drawer (☰ icon)
  3. Tap on “Delete Account”
  4. Confirm the action

What happens next?

  • Your account will be deactivated immediately
  • All personal data will be permanently deleted after 30 days
  • If you log in again within 30 days, your deletion request is cancelled

If you need help, contact us at support@sbsp.in
